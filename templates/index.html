Bridge: Connecting Cultures on Campus



Connect. Collaborate. Cultivate.

Bridge is a social media app designed to connect international communities around colleges in the United States, currently with a specific focus on individuals from the Ex-Yugoslavia region. Our goal is to foster a sense of community, potentially facilitate cultural exchange, and provide valuable networking opportunities. The app combines social features coupled with unique 'Events' and 'Opportunities' pages to enhance user engagement with their community.


Key Features:

  • Global Networking: Connect with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures at colleges nationwide.
  • Follow what students from around the world are doing in their universities
  • Cultural Exchange: Share your traditions, customs, and experiences with peers from around the world.
  • Event Hub: Discover and join multicultural events, workshops, and festivities happening on your campus and beyond.
  • Collaborative Opportunities: Initiate or join projects that promote cultural understanding, tolerance, and collaboration.

Why Bridge?

  • Empowerment: Bridge empowers you to expand your social circle, enrich your college experience, and broaden your worldview.
  • Inclusivity: We believe in building bridges, not walls. Bridge embraces inclusivity, tolerance, and the celebration of differences.
  • Global Perspective: Gain a global perspective without leaving campus. Learn, grow, and thrive in a community that values diversity.
